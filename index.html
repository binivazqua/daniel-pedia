Ashton Dumar Norwill Simmonds[ , mostly known as Daniel Caesar is a Canadian R&B singer and songwriter. His artistry is greatly influenced by soul and gospel, ones which he has integrated along his musical trajectory.

Biography

Ashton Dumar Norwill Simmonds was born on April 5, 1995, in the Scarborough district of Toronto, Ontario, however, he was raised in Oshawa. He is the second of four children to his mother, who takes great protagonism in some of his work , and his father Norwill Simmonds, a Jamaican pastor and gospel singer. Daniel attended to religious schools during his childhood, more specifically, the Seventh Day Adventist Church private institute of Oshawa.

Artistry

Daniel's musical pieces can be resolved in two main pillars: his childhood experiences and love for R&B and electronics.

Referents

In several interviews, Daniel has explicitely stated his admiration and respect for other R&B and gospel singers. He has metioned the following to be his idols:

  • Frank Ocean
  • Kanye West
  • Beyoncé
  • The Doors frontman
  • Jim Morrison

Career

After going thorugh a hard time in his teenage years, Daniel was briefly homeless. He felt no choice but to pursue his calling as a musician. He connected with producers and future collaborators Jordan Evans and Matthew Burnett and began writing and recording.

Highlights

Some of the most notable achievemen'ts during Daniel's first years of career are the following:

  • August 25, 2017: Release of debut album Freudian.
  • 60th Annual Grammy Awards: two nominations for Best R&B Album and Best R&B Performance for "Get You".
  • 61st Annual Grammy Awards: Best R&B Performance for his single "Best Part".
  • April 7, 2023: Third studio album release hrough Republic Records.
